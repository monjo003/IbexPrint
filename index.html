About Us

Ibex Print LLC. Has been a trusted leader in online printing since 2010. We specialize in full color, high quality, fast turnaround digital, offset and large format printing. We proudly offer the lowest possible prices for our printing services while maintaining a high level of quality and professionalism.

We offer substantial savings on all your printing needs, Graphic Design and Mailing Services. We strive for high quality, excellent service, and Savings!

Ibex print occupies a 25,000 square foot stand alone state-of-the-art facility with a 40 inch KBA press. While ensuring high-quality products and brilliant inks, Ibex print is committed to eco-friendly initiatives that support non-profit organizations and environmentally-conscience bus

Our offices and facilities are based in Minneapolis, MN; however we serve clients all over United State.

Its online services allow clients to request a quote, upload a file or design personalized printing pieces. The free, simple yet powerful, online design program saves clients time and money by allowing clients to design a powerful marketing piece in minutes. The client simply selects the pre-designed Standards tab, selects the tab of what they would like printed (business cards, flyers, postcards, etc.), selects the graphics already organized by industry, fills in the text boxes and submits the order. With 10,000 designs available, clients can get the look they want.
